# NewsPredict-
NewsPredict connects news headlines to real-world impact. Get personalized news based on your profession, see historical parallels, and predict ripple effects on markets. Built with React + Node.js + ML for entrepreneurs and professionals who want to understand what news means for their future.
NewsPredict - Understand the Ripple Effect of News


Connect news headlines to real-world impact. Get personalized news based on your profession and predict ripple effects on markets.

🎯 Vision

Most people read news but don't understand how it affects them personally. NewsPredict solves this by:

•
Personalizing news based on your profession (Entrepreneur, Student, Professional, etc.)

•
Showing historical parallels - "Last time this happened, prices went up 10%"

•
Predicting ripple effects - Understanding how global events impact your wallet, career, and business

•
Providing actionable insights - Make better decisions with historical context
